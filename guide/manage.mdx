## \[Detailed Features of the Team Page\]

<img className="block" src="/assets/manage/manage-team.png" />

1. Opens the team selection window, where you can navigate to the team page.
2. **\[Setting & Members\]** leads to the team settings, and **\[New\]** button creates a new project.
3. List of projects managed by the team.

<Note>
  Inviting members to the team and creating projects is available with the
  **Basic plan and above**.
</Note>
